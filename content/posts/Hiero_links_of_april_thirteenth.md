## Hiero Links For The Week Of April 13th

This week featured a lot of new and exciting updates within the Hiero Organization. 
Below are links to some of things that happened this past week. **Check it out!**

---
**Akshat patil** updated the tsc page in the hiero-website by aligning [Read full profile](https://github.com/hiero-ledger/hiero-website/pull/383) buttons at bottom of member cards.

**Manish Dait** improved the documentation for [Ruff and Pre-commit](https://github.com/hiero-ledger/hiero-sdk-python/pull/2155) in the Python SDK to expand upon Ruff Error Suppression, Manual Fixes, and Pre-Commit Manual Execution.

**Dhoke Vinod Eknath** updated cmake_minimum_required [upper policy](https://github.com/hiero-ledger/hiero-sdk-cpp/pull/1447) bound to 4.3 in the C++ SDK for stability and consistency.

**Mustafa Uzun** released an [updated version](https://github.com/hiero-ledger/hiero-sdk-java/pull/2711) of the Java SDK.
