# 📣 📣 📣 Save the Date: Hiero Community Day Comes to Berlin on July 7, 2026 📣 📣 📣

![Hiero Community Day banner](/images/hiero-community-day-2026.png)

**Berlin, Germany — July 7, 2026**

The Hiero community is coming together in Berlin for a full day of collaboration, learning, and connection and we want you to be part of it!

The event is scheduled for **Tuesday, July 7, 2026** at **TreeHouse Berlin** in **Berlin, Germany**. We invite developers, contributors, adopters, and open source users to join us in-person.

This will be a full-day designed to bring the Hiero maintainers, contributors and community together and give attendees a closer look at the technologies, ideas, and people shaping the future of decentralized systems.

## Why attend?

Hiero Community Day is a chance to meet the people building across the Hiero ecosystem, learn directly from contributors and maintainers, and explore what is coming next for the project.

Whether you are already involved in Hiero or are just beginning to explore decentralized ledger technology, this event is a great opportunity to connect with the community and get the latest insight into the work happening by the community.

During the event, we will cover topics on **Agentic AI**, **Updates on Hiero SDKs**, **Proof of Personhood**, **Core component updates** including Consensus Node, Solo, and Solo Action, and more!

## Event details

**When:** Tuesday, July 7, 2026  
**Where:** TreeHouse Berlin, Berlin, Germany  
**Format:** In-person only  
**Admission:** Free to attend  
**Included:** Food and cold drinks  

The event will run throughout the day and into the evening, giving attendees time to learn, ask questions, exchange ideas, and build connections across the Hiero community.

## Join us before WeAreDevelopers World Congress

Hiero Community Day takes place right before [**WeAreDevelopers World Congress**](https://www.wearedevelopers.com/), making it a great opportunity for attendees already traveling to Berlin to extend their plans and spend a day with the Hiero community.

If you are planning to attend WeAreDevelopers 2026, we encourage you to join us for Hiero Community Day as well and continue learning more about the open source technologies powering the Hiero ecosystem.

## Learn more about Hiero

Hiero is an open source project under [**LF Decentralized Trust**](https://www.lfdecentralizedtrust.org/projects/hiero) focused on building decentralized ledger technologies and supporting the ecosystem around them. Developers and contributors can explore the project, repositories, and current work on [GitHub hiero-ledger organization](https://github.com/hiero-ledger/)

More details about the agenda, speakers, and sessions will be shared soon.

**Save the date and join us in Berlin on July 7, 2026. We look forward to seeing you!**
