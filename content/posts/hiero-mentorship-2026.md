**📢 Calling all Hiero community members and aspiring contributors! 📢**

We have a new opportunity to get involved and learn more about the Hiero project through the **LF Decentralized Trust Mentorship Programs**.

We are excited to share that **four Hiero mentorship opportunities are currently open for mentee applications**. These mentorships are a great way to gain hands-on experience, work closely with project maintainers and community leaders, and contribute to the growth of the [Hiero project](https://github.com/hiero-ledger).

## 🚀 Open Hiero Mentorships

Applications are now open for the following mentorship opportunities:

- [Extending Hiero Enterprise Java with Smart Contract Event and Log APIs](https://mentorship.lfx.linuxfoundation.org/project/9afaacd2-76ad-4758-8ae7-c0d4a2823f9a)
- [Hiero Github Workflow App](https://mentorship.lfx.linuxfoundation.org/project/179d1abb-3600-4f7a-a55d-342e7c9921b4)
- [Contributor Identity Verification Prototype](https://mentorship.lfx.linuxfoundation.org/project/64c64daa-ffdb-4871-82f5-01c1bdc7fecc)
- [Designing the Next-Generation Hiero SDK Architecture (SDK V3)](https://mentorship.lfx.linuxfoundation.org/project/b4cec9b4-57a5-494d-ab09-eeec7c29b85d)

Each mentorship offers an opportunity to learn in the open, collaborate directly with experienced maintainers, and improve your skills.

## 📝 How to Apply

If you are interested in applying as a mentee, start by reviewing the official LFX Mentorship documentation:

- [LFX Mentorship Overview](https://docs.linuxfoundation.org/lfx/mentorship)
- [LFX Mentorship Guide for Mentees](https://docs.linuxfoundation.org/lfx/mentorship/mentees)

These resources explain how to create your mentee profile, review program requirements, and apply for a mentorship program of your interest.

## 🗓️ Join the Hiero Mentorship Call

We also invite the community to join the **Hiero Mentorship Call**, held every **Monday at 8:00 AM PST**.

This meeting is open to mentors and mentees and is a space to bring questions, discuss ideas, and get support related to the mentorship program process.

You can access the calendar [here](https://zoom-lfx.platform.linuxfoundation.org/meetings/hiero?view=week)

## 🧭 What’s The Next 2026 Mentorship Process Dates?

For those interested in the broader mentorship process and upcoming deadlines for 2026's mentorship program process, the full **2026 LF Decentralized Trust mentorship schedule** is available [here](https://lf-decentralized-trust-mentorships.github.io/mentorship-program/main/)

This includes the current application window, review period, mentee selection timeline, onboarding, and the full mentor/mentee working period for 2026.

## 👉 Join the Conversation

Want to connect with the community and continue the discussion? Join us on [Discord!](https://discord.com/invite/hyperledger)

Whether you are planning to apply, considering becoming a mentor in the future, or simply want to learn more about LFX Mentorship and Hiero, we encourage you to get involved.

We look forward to seeing new contributors join the Hiero ecosystem through this year’s mentorship program!
